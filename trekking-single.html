Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking

$55.00 USD /person (minimum 2 persons)

Mount Batur Ititerary

  • 01.30 - 03.00 AM: Pick up time on your hotel.
  • 03.45 AM: Arrived at Toya Bungkah, introduction to our experiences guide and briefing about safety procedure before trekking
  • 06.15 AM: Arrival at the summit of Mount Batur (sunrise point).
  • 07.00 AM: Continue our trek around the entire crater.
  • 08.00 AM: Begin our descent to the finishing point of our trek (Toyabungkah village).
  • 09.00 AM: Arrival at Toyabungkah village and board car.
  • 11.30 AM: Stop off at a Balinese Coffee plantation (optional).
  • 01.30 PM: Return arrival at your hotel.

Inclution

  1. Pick up and return to your hotel with air conditioner car
  2. Entrance fee to kintamani area and parking fee
  3. Experience private professional trekking guides with good English
  4. Breakfast, mineral water and hot drink

You Should Bring

  1. Sport shoes
  2. backpack
  3. glove
  4. long pant
  5. camera
  6. jacket
